"Hello!Welcome to Fight Club Gym"

Myanmar Fight Team (MTF)

Welcome to the Myanmar Fight Team, where you can train like a fighter and achieve your fitness goals. We offer classes for all levels, from novice to professional, so that everyone can get in shape and learn the skills of boxing and martial arts. Our classes are taught by expert coaches who have years of experience in the sport and are dedicated to helping you reach your full potential. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, improve your stamina, or simply learn a new skill, our classes can help you achieve your goal. At Myanmar Fight Team, we offer a range of classes to suit your needs and schedule. Our classes include Kickboxing, Muay Thai, Jiu-Jitsu, and Mixed Martial Arts, among others. Each class is designed to challenge you and help you develop your skills in a safe and supportive environment. At Myanmar Fight Team, we believe that everyone has the potential to be a fighter. Whether you're looking to get in shape, learn a new skill, or compete at the highest level, our gym is the perfect place to train. If you're looking for a gym that will challenge you and help you achieve your fitness goals, then look no further than Myanmar Fight Team. Join us today and start your journey towards becoming a fighter.
